To protect yourself during the outbreak of COVID-19, this app helps you keeps your hands off your face using your webcam and machine learning. We take your privacy very seriously. All of the camera data stays local and never leaves your computer. This app works locally in your browser.

FAQ

How does this work under the hood?

Using Google's handpose and facemesh packages (we combined the two), we're able to detect when you're touching your face based on the intersection of your hand and face.

What are its limitations?

The models are relatively new and provide great overall accuracy, but there are a couple drawbacks and you may need to tweak a couple things for the best experience:

  • Handpose currently only supports recognizing a single hand.
  • There can be false positive notifications if a hand is detected when one isn't actually present.
  • As a camera is 2D there can also be false positives present when placing the hand in front of the face (but not touching) in 3D space.
  • For desktop notifications, please ensure that you have do not disturb turned off in your notification center!
  • This app does not currently run in the background; please keep this tab in a separate window and keep it open.
